# Technical Issues in Cloud

- Failure of service
- Problem with internet bandwidth
- Data recovery and backup
- Loss of bankruptcy of service provider
- Improper cloud service exit policy
- Vendor lock-in
- Multi-tenancy
- Virtualization

## SaaS Issues

- Data access risk
- Instability
- Lack of transparency
- Identity theft
- Uncertainty of your data's location
- Paying upfront and long-term
- No direct control over your own data
- The service may not keep up with modern security standards

- Open or proprietary
- Software compatibility
- Support availability and cost
- Platform management

- Access controls
- Energy resource management
- Multi tenancy
- Server consolidation
