## Missing Statement and Objectives

- Missing statements
	- Establishes the purpose of the database
	- Provides a distinct focus and purpose
	- Focused on database developer, database, and management personnel
- Mission objectives
	- Statements that represent the general tasks users can perform
	- Focused on end users
