# The dot product: Measuring vector alignment

- Dot product ($u \cdot v$)
	- Inner product
	- 90° => $u \cdot v = 0$
	- $u \cdot v = |u| \cdot |v| \cdot cos(\theta)$

# The cross product: Measuring oriented area

- Cross product ($u \times v$)
- $u \times v = (u_yy_z - u_zv_y, u_zy_x - u_xv_z, u_xy_z - u_yv_x)$
